Movie poster series

A growing collection of original movie posters — a mix of personal concept pieces and real short-film commissions — exploring typography, mood, and composition across genres.

Role
Designer
Tools
Photoshop, Illustrator, Procreate
Color palette

The work

Each poster starts from the film's mood rather than its plot — a color, a texture, a single image that carries the tone before any text goes on the page.
